An exciting new partnership!

Pat's Salmon Cookies has recently entered into a licensing partnership with Uncle Ike's and The Goodship Company to begin producing and selling the world's first salmon-pot cookie. They are entirely locally sourced and produced, soon to become a local legend!

Pat's Salmon Cookies Voted Best In Seattle!

In a recent poll by The Stranger, Pat's Salmon Cookies was recently voted the most fish-tasting confection in Seattle. As most Seattleites are well aware, this coveted top spot has been held for years by Bill's Bass Brownies until last fall's unsolved arson.
